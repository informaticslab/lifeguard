Privacy Information: This section explains the overall function of Lifeguard and provides detailed instructions on how it can be enabled or disabled.

Show Device ID: This feature displays the unique identifier of the specific device using Lifeguard. This identifier will not change over time. This identifier also provides a link between the device and the CDC staff member using the device.

Email Device ID: This feature allows the CDC staff member to easily share the unique identifier of their specific device with the EOC.

Call EOC: This feature allows a CDC staff member to easily contact the EOC for support and guidance.

Send Location Now: When used, this feature will allow the current location of the device to be immediately transmitted to the EOC.
